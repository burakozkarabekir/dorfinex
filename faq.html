Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and engagement models

General Questions

What is Dorfinex?
Dorfinex is a digital consulting and technology partnership firm that helps enterprises, service providers, and technology vendors achieve measurable growth through strategic consulting, ecosystem partnerships, and technology innovation. We specialize in digital transformation, AI/analytics, telecom/ICT solutions, and go-to-market strategies.
What industries do you serve?
We primarily serve telecommunications, technology, enterprise software, and digital platform companies. Our expertise spans across B2B and B2C sectors, including service providers, SaaS vendors, hardware manufacturers, system integrators, and innovative startups across various verticals.
Where are you located?
Dorfinex operates with a flexible, remote-first model to best serve our global clientele. We work with clients remotely and on-site based on project requirements, ensuring seamless collaboration regardless of location.

Technical Questions

What is your typical project process?
Our proven process follows four key phases: (1) Discovery & Assessment (1-2 weeks) - we analyze your current state, goals, and constraints, (2) Strategy & Planning (1-2 weeks) - we develop a detailed roadmap with clear milestones and KPIs, (3) Execution & Implementation (2-12 weeks) - hands-on delivery with your team, including training and knowledge transfer, (4) Optimization & Support (ongoing) - we measure results and provide ongoing guidance to ensure sustainable success.
How quickly can you start?
We can typically begin new projects within 1-2 weeks of contract signature. For urgent initiatives, we can start initial discovery and planning within 3-5 business days. Our team is structured to be flexible and responsive, allowing us to scale resources based on your timeline requirements. We'll always provide a realistic timeline during our initial consultation.
How do you measure success?
Success is measured through clear, agreed-upon KPIs established during project planning. Common metrics include: Revenue growth, Customer acquisition cost reduction, Operational efficiency improvements, Time-to-market acceleration, Partnership deal velocity, Technology adoption rates, and ROI achievement. We provide regular progress reports and adjust strategies based on real performance data to ensure we're delivering measurable value.
What technologies do you specialize in?
We have deep expertise across cloud platforms, data & analytics, AI/ML frameworks, CRM/ERP systems, MarTech stacks, telecom technologies, and modern development stacks including microservices, containers, and serverless architectures. We're technology-agnostic and recommend solutions based on your specific context and requirements.
Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?
Yes. We offer post-project support through various models: (1) Extended Support - fixed period of assistance after go-live, (2) Retainer Agreements - ongoing advisory and troubleshooting, (3) On-Demand Support - pay-as-you-go help when needed, (4) Managed Services - we operate and optimize on your behalf. Our goal is to ensure long-term success, not just project completion.

Services & Solutions

What services do you offer?
Our core services include: Digital Consulting (growth strategy, GTM plans, funnel optimization), Partnerships & Ecosystem Development, Tech Advisory (vendor selection, architecture review), AI & Analytics (ML implementation, data strategy), Telecom & ICT Solutions (5G, network monitoring), and MarTech Solutions (automation, customer journey orchestration).
How do you approach digital transformation projects?
We follow a pragmatic, outcome-focused approach: (1) Discovery & Assessment - understand current state and business goals, (2) Strategy Development - create actionable roadmap with clear KPIs, (3) Execution - hands-on implementation with your team, (4) Optimization - continuous measurement and improvement. We emphasize quick wins while building long-term capabilities.
Do you only provide strategy or also implementation?
We provide both. Unlike traditional consulting firms that stop at strategy, we roll up our sleeves and execute alongside your team. Our engagements can range from pure advisory to full end-to-end implementation, depending on your needs. We believe the best strategies are those that get implemented successfully.
Can you help with technology vendor selection?
Absolutely. Our Tech Advisory service includes comprehensive vendor evaluation based on your specific requirements. We are vendor-agnostic and provide unbiased recommendations. We assess technical capabilities, pricing models, integration complexity, support quality, and long-term viability to help you make informed decisions.

Engagement & Pricing

What are your engagement models?
We offer flexible engagement models: (1) Project-Based - fixed scope and timeline with defined deliverables, (2) Retainer - ongoing advisory and support on monthly basis, (3) Time & Materials - flexible resource allocation billed hourly/daily, (4) Success-Based - performance-linked fees tied to measurable outcomes. We tailor our approach to your specific needs and risk profile.
How long does a typical engagement last?
Engagement duration varies based on scope. Strategic assessments typically take 4-8 weeks. Digital transformation programs range from 3-12 months. Partnership development projects span 2-6 months. We can also provide ongoing advisory relationships that last multiple years. We'll provide a clear timeline during the scoping phase.
How much do your services cost?
Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, duration, and required expertise. We provide transparent proposals with detailed cost breakdowns tailored to your specific needs. We offer flexible engagement models and payment terms that can be structured to align with your budget and cash flow requirements. Contact us for a customized quote based on your requirements.
Do you work with startups?
Yes! We work with innovative startups, especially in technology and digital platform sectors. For early-stage companies, we offer flexible pricing models including equity-based arrangements, milestone-based payments, and reduced rates for promising ventures. We're passionate about helping innovators scale and can provide valuable introductions to investors and potential partners.

Process & Timeline

What is your typical project process?
Our process follows these phases: (1) Initial Consultation - understand your needs and challenges, (2) Proposal & Scoping - detailed SOW with timelines and deliverables, (3) Kickoff - align team, set expectations, establish communication channels, (4) Discovery - data gathering, stakeholder interviews, market research, (5) Analysis & Strategy - develop recommendations and roadmap, (6) Execution - implement solutions with your team, (7) Measurement - track KPIs and optimize, (8) Knowledge Transfer - ensure sustainability.
How quickly can you start?
For urgent needs, we can mobilize within 1-2 weeks. Standard projects typically kick off within 2-4 weeks from agreement signature. We prioritize quick starts while ensuring proper planning and resource allocation. During peak seasons, lead times may be slightly longer, but we'll always communicate realistic timelines upfront.
How do you measure success?
Success is measured through clearly defined KPIs agreed upon at project start. Common metrics include: revenue growth, cost reduction, customer acquisition cost, conversion rate improvement, time-to-market reduction, partner deals closed, system uptime/performance, user adoption rates, and ROI. We provide regular progress reports with data-backed insights and work collaboratively with your team to adjust strategies based on results.
